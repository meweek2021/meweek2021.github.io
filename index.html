What is M&E Week?

An event celebrated annually every third week of June by virtue of Presidential Proclamation No. 144, series of 2011. The 1st National M&E Conference, to be held during the 2022 M&E Week celebration, provides a platform for researchers, scientists, and engineers from the industry, government research institutes, and academe to present and discuss the recent development and progress made in the following fields:

Submission of Papers

Accepted full paper will be invited to give an oral presentation at the conference and be published in full text in Philippine Metals (ISSN 0115-117) or Philippine Journal of Science (ISSN 0031-7683) Special Edition. The authors have the right not to allow the publication of their papers in the said journal.

Procedure for Paper Submission
1. Authors are required to submit an abstract of 250 words (maximum)
2. The authors will prepare a slide presentation, preferably in PowerPoint, for presentation on an online platform. Only one of the authors, preferably the principal author, is allowed to present the paper where the length of the presentation shall not exceed 15 minutes.
3. When the author/s wish to have their paper published in the Philippine Metals or Philippine Journal of Science special edition, they shall submit a publication-ready manuscript
