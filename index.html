What is M&E Week?

An event celebrated annually every third week of June by virtue of Presidential Proclamation No. 144, series of 2011. The 1st National M&E Conference, to be held during the 2022 M&E Week celebration, provides a platform for researchers, scientists, and engineers from the industry, government research institutes, and academe to present and discuss the recent development and progress made in the following fields:

Submission of Papers

As part of the celebration of the 2022 M&E Week, the DOST-Metals Industry Research and Development Center will hold the 1st National M&E Conference, an avenue for the presentation and discussion of recent development and progress made in the following fields:

• Metalworking (machining, cutting, joining)
• Metal casting
• Metal forming (rolling, forging, bending, extrusion)
• Heat Treatment
• Surface Engineering
• Additive Manufacturing
• Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
• Machine Development for Agricultural, Industrial, Transport, Health and Defense applications
• Metal Degradation and Failure Analysis
• Metal Behavior and Characterization
• Industry 4.0 and Smart Manufacturing Systems
• Process Automation (Smart and Green Agricultural)
• Iron and Steelmaking technologies
• Manufacture of non-ferrous metals and alloys
• Emerging Technologies in Metals and Engineering
• Trends in the Metals and Engineering Industry

We are calling on all researchers, scientists, and engineers from the industry, public and private research institutes, and the academe to be part of the 1st National M&E Conference.

Dates to remember:
May 15, 2022 - deadline for submission of the Abstract (250 words)
May 27, 2022 - deadline for submission of a full paper of accepted entries
June 15, 2022 - presentation of the research during the 1st National M&E Conference
