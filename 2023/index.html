What is M&E Week?

An event celebrated annually every third week of June by virtue of Presidential Proclamation No. 144, series of 2011. The 2nd National M&E Conference will be held on 20 June 2023 at Titanium Auditorium, MIRDC, Gen. Santos Ave., Bicutan, Taguig City.

About

The Metals and Engineering Week is an annual event that recognizes the ever-growing importance of the metals, engineering, and allied industries to nation-building.
